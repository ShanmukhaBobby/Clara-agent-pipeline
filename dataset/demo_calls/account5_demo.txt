We provide services including general medical consultation,

If a caller describes an emergency condition
such as severe chest pain, breathing difficulty,
or heavy bleeding,
the call should be transferred immediately to the triage nurse.

If the triage nurse cannot be reached,
the caller must be advised to seek immediate emergency care.

Appointment scheduling requests should be forwarded
to the front desk staff.
